Pasta Salad on a Stick

Description

Salad on Sticks -Cold Tortellini Salad Kabobs are a mouth-watering pasta salad served simply, on a stick. They are the perfect snack to serve.These Cold Tortellini Skewers add a little something extra special to any event you bring them to. The best part? No utensils needed!

Ingredients

  • 8 cheese tortellinis
  • 8 slices thinly sliced salami
  • 8 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 2 artichoke hearts, quartered
  • 4 grape tomatoes, halved
  • 8 (1 inch) cubes fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 4 pitted black olives, halved
  • 8 bamboo toothpicks

Steps

  1. Bring a small pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook tortellini in the boiling water until pasta floats to the top, 1 to 2 minutes; drain.
  2. Thread 1 tortellini, 1 salami slice, 1 sprig parsley, 1 artichoke heart quarter, 1 tomato half, 1 mozzarella cheese cube, and 1 olive half, respectively, onto a toothpick. Repeat threading with remaining toothpicks.
